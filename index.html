About

Donate Land is an independently owned and family run business that specialises in creation of artisan donuts. Our donuts are freshly prepared,each day prior to sunrise.Currently we make 75 different types of donuts on a daily basis and have our stores in Rome,Cannes and in Werneck. Definitely pop by one of our locations to try at least one of our donuts but if you can't make it-don't worry! We've recently introduced both a catering service for special occasions and have also recently introduced a delivery service for our 'Surpise Donuts Collection'box which contains 6 different donut flavours.
